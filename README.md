# ASEAN Quantum Summit 2025 — UTM (Event Recap)

Grateful to have attended the ASEAN Quantum Summit 2025, held from **10–12 December 2025** at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), Johor Bahru.

The summit provided valuable insights into the growing landscape of quantum technologies, highlighting regional collaboration, research advancements, and future opportunities across ASEAN. It was inspiring to engage with experts, researchers, and policymakers discussing how quantum innovation will shape technology and society.

We were also honoured by the presence of a guest of honour from Johor who officiated this inaugural ASEAN-level platform, marking a significant step toward strengthening ASEAN’s readiness in quantum technology.

A meaningful learning experience beyond the classroom. ✨

## Highlights
- Dates: 10–12 December 2025  
- Venue: Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), Johor Bahru  
- Focus: Quantum technologies, research collaboration, policy & regional readiness  
- Attendees: Academics, students, researchers, industry practitioners, and policymakers  
- Special note: Officiated by a guest of honour representing Johor state leadership
